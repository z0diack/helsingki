Getting There

The festival area is located nearby the centre of Helsinki, easily accessible by public transportation, bike or foot. We recommend the use of public transportation or bicycling when coming to the festival.

Bike locking stations are available in mass at the entrance of the festival and free bike locks are provided to encourage the use of bikes!

Street address: Parrukatu, 00540 Helsinki.

PUBLIC TRANSPORTATION

The festival site is located in the public transportation zone A. In zone A you need an AB, ABC or ABCD ticket, depending on the area you travel in. A one-zone ticket is available only in zone D.

Read more about the zones here.

    The closest stations and stops:
  • Metro – Sörnäinen
  • Bus – Suvilahti and Sörnäinen (M)
  • Tram – Karhupuisto, Kaarlenkatu, Urheilutalo and Helsinginkatu

For more detailed information about public transportation, please visit The Helsinki Region journey planner (Reittiopas) by HSL.

In addition to regular services, HSL will organise additional night services on Helsinki metro during the festival weekend. The additional metro trains will operate every ten minutes to Vuosaari and Tapiola, and every 20 minutes to Mellunmäki and Matinkylä. Traveling tickets can be purchased from ticket vending machines, HSL app, parking ticket machines, R-Kioski kiosks, HSL service points and bus drivers. Bus drivers only accept cash and they do not have to accept notes larger than 20 EUR. Check all the ticket selling points, ticket machines and service point locations here.

Driving

Although we would recommend finding public transport or biking to the festival if it is possible but there is parking around the corner at Parkkipaikka which can be used for 24h for free!

Street Address: Brädgårdsgatan, 00580 Helsinki

HSL APP

HSL’s tickets, Journey Planner and transport information tailored specifically for you — all in one app!

Created by the goverment at Helsinki it will be able to help you travel around with ease during your time in the city!

The HSL app is available for Android and Apple iOS devices free of charge. Download here

TAXI

You can order a taxi by calling +358 100 0700. Taxis in Finland are reliable and always run by meter.

Beware if you are not from Finland charges may be applied when calling this number!

A temporary taxi stand is located opposite the wristband exchange point, across the Sörnäisten Rantatie street. Please note that it is not possible to order taxis elsewhere in the Suvilahti area as all taxis leave from the temporary taxi stand.
