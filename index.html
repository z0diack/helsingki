HelSINGki Music Festival

HelSINGki Festival is an award winning multi-genre music festival in Helsinki. Started in 2006 as a small alternate music event, HelSINGki has grown over its fifteen years into one of northern Europe's most significant music festivals. Every year the festival puts together an enviable lineup mixing some of the most respected names from a broad spectrum of genres with younger acts making waves. The legendary music festival is back for its 15th edition on the 25th - 27th September 2021!

The multi-award-winning HelSINGki festival are returning for a weekend long extravaganza set in the breath taking Suvilahti, a former powerplant that comprises of nine buildings, two gasometers and a spaceous yard space.
